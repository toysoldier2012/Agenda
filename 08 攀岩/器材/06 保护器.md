## Tuber/ATC/Reverso

其实只有Petzl的Tuber被称为Reverso

![[Pasted image 20250624225221.png|165]]

Black diamond称为ATC

![[Pasted image 20250624225312.png|161]]

Mammut称其为Alpine Belaying Device

![[Pasted image 20250624225250.png|175]]
